Key Features

What Makes This System Smart

Each layer of the pipeline is engineered for accuracy and fairness.

📊

Multi-Feature Analysis

Analyzes 14+ applicant features including income type, education, employment history, family status, and housing to build a holistic risk profile.

🧠

4 Competing ML Models

Trains Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, and XGBoost simultaneously — selects the champion by F1-score on a held-out test set.

💳

Credit History Signals

Aggregates monthly credit records per applicant — EMI paid-off count, overdue flags, and account window — to assess repayment behavior.

🎯

Hyperparameter Tuning

RandomizedSearchCV explores hundreds of parameter combinations to push accuracy beyond the baseline and reduce overfitting.

🔐

Consistent Encoding

Persists all LabelEncoders via Pickle — guaranteeing the exact same categorical transformations at prediction time as during training.

☁️

Cloud-Ready

Optional IBM Watson Machine Learning deployment block included — enabling scalable, real-time cloud-based prediction beyond the local Flask server.
